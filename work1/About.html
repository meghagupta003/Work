About Us

Tudor World is a living history museum situated in the center of the street on Stratford-upon-Avon.

It is an one-of-its-kind experience with various events, artifacts, and a team of enthusiastic actors that will have you live the life of a Tudor citizen in no time.

Tudor World also offers a variety of tours across the area, such as Shakespeare tour - his hometown. You get to visit the places, and experience the life of the Shakespeare, visit his birthplace, school, and final resting place.

Tudor World is undoubtedly a must visit attraction point in Stratford- upon-Avon.

Background

Tudor World is run and owned by a husband-wife duo, Janet and John Ford, who took over the museum in 2007.

The museum is solely run by the visitors, without any grants or funding from the external sources.

This is one of the main reasons why the team always try to bring in new fun and punch to keep the visitors coming.
