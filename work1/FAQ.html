Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is the museum suitable for children visits?

Yes, it is suitable for children.
It has a variety of exciting elements that children loves.

2. Why there is a charge to visit the museum?

There is a minimal charge for visit to the museum.
The museum has no external funding, so public support is all it has.

3. Can we take pictures and videos inside the museum?

Yes, photography and videography are allowed inside the museum.
We request you to not touch anything inside the museum.

4. How long does it take to visit the museum?

The time to visit the museum depends upon the tours you take.
It offers different events and shows on different days.

5. Can we eat, drink, or smoke inside the museum?

You cannot take outside items inside the museum.
It has a separate area outside where you can have your food and drinks.
